Pokemon Trainer Red

Pokemon trainer Red is quite possibly the second most fammous human in the Pokemon universe, hanging behind Ash Ketchum simply because of the anime.

According to canon, Red is the name of the main character of the Generation 1 games set out in Kanto. His very first appearance came in the original Pokemon Video Games, Red, Blue, Green, and Yellow.

Since then he has become somewhat of a Pokemon legend among the playerbase being regarded by most as "the Greatest Trainer Ever" or "the True Pokemon Master." He has made a couple appearances in later games such as HeartGold and SoulSilver, and Pokemon Sun and Moon. Where he is known to always command a scary strong higher level team of classic generation 1 Pokemon. He also was the main character in the generation 1 Pokemon Manga "Pokemon Adventures" which told a different story then the video games but continued the legend of Red being the best ever.
